How It Works
Step 01
Motion Vector Extraction
Farneback dense optical flow tracks every pixel's displacement between consecutive frames.
Step 02
Direction Entropy
Shannon entropy over 8 directional bins — calm crowds move together, panicking crowds scatter randomly.
Step 03
Crowd Density
MOG2 background subtraction isolates foreground pixels to estimate how densely packed a crowd is.
Step 04
Multi-Signal Fusion
Speed, entropy, density, acceleration, turbulence, and divergence are weighted and combined into a 0–100 panic score.
